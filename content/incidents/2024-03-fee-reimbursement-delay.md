Protests were reported at multiple degree and engineering colleges across Hyderabad in March 2024 after fee reimbursement payments for the 2023–24 academic year were not credited to eligible students by the announced date.

Students from BC, SC, and ST categories — who rely on fee reimbursement to cover tuition costs — staged demonstrations outside college gates and at district collectorate offices. The Education Department acknowledged the delay and attributed it to a backlog in beneficiary verification following a system migration.

The department issued a statement that payments would be released in phases within 30 days. As of the time of reporting, no official release date had been confirmed.
