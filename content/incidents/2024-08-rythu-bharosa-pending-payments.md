Farmers in Karimnagar, Nalgonda, and Warangal districts staged protests in late August 2024 after Rythu Bharosa Kharif 2024 investment support payments — promised at ₹15,000 per year — had not been credited despite the Kharif sowing season being underway.

The government stated that beneficiary database verification was ongoing after it found duplicate and ghost entries from the previous administration's records. Agriculture Department officials were quoted saying payments would begin after verification, but no specific timeline was given.

Opposition parties alleged that the delay was fiscal in nature and pointed to the state's debt burden. The government denied this. Given conflicting official statements, this entry is graded **Multiple Sources** rather than **Official Record** — no government order confirming a specific disbursement timeline was found.
