The Telangana Contractors Association formally wrote to the state government in February 2025 stating that pending bills for completed government works exceeded ₹8,000 crore. The association stated that work on several road, irrigation, and municipal projects had been halted due to non-payment.

The Finance Department acknowledged outstanding payments but stated that the state was managing a challenging fiscal position inherited from the previous administration. Officials said bills were being cleared in order of priority.

This incident is graded **Single Source** as the ₹8,000 crore figure has been reported by a single outlet (The Hindu) and could not be independently verified at the time of entry. The Contractors Association claim is treated as **Allegation** pending official confirmation.

> **[Allegation]** The ₹8,000 crore figure is from the Contractors Association; not confirmed by government audit.
