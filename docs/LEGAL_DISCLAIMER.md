## Nature of Content

- All content is based on publicly available sources. Links to original sources are included for independent verification.

## Corrections

We actively incorporate corrections. If you believe any entry is factually incorrect, please [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/your-org/tsgov/issues/new) with:
- The specific claim you believe is wrong
- A credible source supporting the correction

Corrections are reviewed within 7 days and incorporated transparently — old versions are not deleted.
